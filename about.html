Our History

The Strathmore University Table Tennis Club (SUTTC) was founded in 2010 with a mission to promote table tennis within the university and the wider community. Over the years, SUTTC has grown into a powerhouse, hosting annual tournaments that attract players from across Kenya. Our club has produced numerous champions and fostered a vibrant community of table tennis enthusiasts.

The 2025 Tournament

The 2025 SUTTC Table Tennis Tournament is set to be our biggest yet! Taking place at Strathmore University, this year’s event will feature categories for all skill levels: Under 18 (Boys & Girls), Singles (Men & Ladies), Mixed Team (2 Men & 2 Ladies), and Family Team (Amateur Division). With over 100 expected participants, exciting prizes, and a chance to make history, this is an event you don’t want to miss.

Our Mission & Values

At SUTTC, we believe in the power of sport to unite, inspire, and transform. Our mission is to provide a platform for players to showcase their talent, build lasting connections, and grow both on and off the table. We value sportsmanship, inclusivity, and excellence, and we’re committed to making table tennis accessible to everyone.
